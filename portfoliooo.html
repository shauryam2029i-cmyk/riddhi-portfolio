Project 01

The Artifact Collection

"This concept explores beauty not as perfection, but as time made visible. Ancient artefacts from Egypt, Rome, and the Middle East become metaphors for how history leaves traces: ancient pottery, sculptures, and age-defining cracks that turn objects into living archives of touch, ritual, and memory. The palette of worn browns, lapis blues, molten gold, and carnelian reds channels Galliano's 2004 Dior couture while echoing the stones, metals, and pigments that once adorned temples and bodies."

Project 02

Nazar No Entry

"Nazar No Entry takes the classic evil eye, nimbu-mirchi, and all those desi 'protection hacks' and gives them a glow-up. The lemons have attitude. The chillies have personality. The eyes are basically saying, 'Look at me, but don’t you dare nazar me.' It’s fun, exaggerated, filmy, and 100% Indian inspired by the same energy with which dadi waves mirchis around your head like she’s performing a personal exorcism."

Project 03

Tangled Bags

"This is a collection of handbags I created with a whimsical theme inspired by Disney's Tangled. The movie is very close to my heart, and I resonate deeply with the character Rapunzel, so the inspiration came from my desire to express my inner child through this work. The target audience is people like me who enjoy detailed, colorful, and almost maximalist accessories."

Project 04

Woven Fairytale

"This woven belt blends marine beauty with mythic charm. A stylized mermaid forms the central motif, framed by waves, fish, and an oyster symbolizing hidden treasures. Together, the elements create a fluid, storytelling pattern that brings the magic and mystery of the ocean to a wearable textile."

Project 05

Schiaparelli Collection

"This collection is inspired by Schiaparelli's surrealist legacy and Elsa's collaborations with the legendary artist Salvador Dalí. I explored techniques like resin casting, smocking, and gold thread embroidery to reimagine surrealism in a way that felt approachable to me."
